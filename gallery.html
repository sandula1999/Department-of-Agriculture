1.Benefits of Central Provincial Agricultural Development to Farmers

Parallel to the celebration of two years in position of the honorable Governor to the Central Province, on the 2021/12/15, the District Deputy Director of agriculture (Kandy) office distributed the benefits to the farmers who are the beneficiaries of the development programmes conducted in Agriculture Development Plan - 2021. Hon. governor to the Central Province, hon. Chairman of Medadumbara P.S. , the coordinating secretary of the hon. governor , secretary to the governor , secretary to d Agriculture ministry , Deputy chief secretary (administration ) ,provincial director of agriculture and his staff were attended to this occasion ... 25 students of NVQ (level 4) who passed out from the Nawayalathenna District Agriculture Training Centre were also given certificates at this occasion.





2.Benefits for the Fisheries Community in the Central Province for the Development Program 2021

This year, under the strict traveling restrictions and health guidance enforced due to the spread of Covid 19 epidemic all over the world , a “tree planting programme” was conducted at the Agriculture Training Centre premises in Nawayalathenna to mark the celebration of the Environment Day -2021. The Chief Secretary to the Central provincial Council, Mr.Gamini Rajarathne , The Secretary to the Provincial Ministry of Agriculture, Mrs. L.A. Sujatha Wijesinghe, the Provincial Director of Agriculture Mr.Ranathunga Bandara ,Kandy district Deputy Director (Agriculture), Farm manager of Nawayalathenna Agriculture Training Centre, and Officials from the provincial Ministry of Agriculture were participated at this event On this special day, the United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) is planning to draw the attention of the entire world on the necessity of protecting the environment under the theme of “Ecosystem Restoration” – Reimagine, Recreate, Restore





3.Developing Public Places in the Central Province in an Eco-friendly Manner

The step taken by the Central Province Governor Lalith u Gamage on 05.08.2021 to plant named “magul karandha” at the Peradeniya Railway Station premises as the first step in the active environmental program aimed at developing public places in the Central Province in an eco-friendly manner.
